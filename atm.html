Overall Reflection of ATM

I am so glad that I took and have been taking ATM for my elective during middle school. It has taught me amazing skills that will be very helpful in the future, like typing, coding, and using microsoft programs. I know that this will be helpful in the future when I am working on important assignments in school or even work. These skills will last me forever and I am so grateful that I have had this opportunity. I have made friends in this program and learned so much than I would have in any other program about computers at this school.
