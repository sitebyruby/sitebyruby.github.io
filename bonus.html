Self Defense

Karate is a huge part of my life, and has been for almost 6 years now. I train at Z Ultimate Self Defense studios in Long Beach, and it has changed my life. I have made friends that will last a lifetime, and gained mentors that I can look up to, and talk to whenever I need them. It has been the best years of my life.

It's amazing to think that some people may not even see the point to learning self defense, but it could save a life, even several lives, and it will. If you know self defense, and you're in a difficult situation, it can help you get out of it. It also helps you learn manners and discipline.

I admit, karate isn't for everyone, and some people think that it's just for kids, to get interested in something when they are young, and then move on to a different sport. I don't feel this way though. I think that people should join karate and learn self defense because it is so helpful to know. I don't know how to explain how much it has affected my life. I have a family there, and so many different oppourtunities. For example one day, when I have a work permit, I could have a part time job there.
